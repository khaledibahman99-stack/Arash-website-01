About the Makers

Noise Collections and Dova are two sister brands, born from the hands and hearts of two makers in Tabriz.

Arash Khavari – Industrial Designer (Noise Collections)

Arash is a graduate of Industrial Design from Tabriz University of Art, where he also runs his workshop (No. 26). With a deep love for materials and craftsmanship, he designs and hand-makes functional objects — from fabric pencil cases to leather key holders — under the brand Noise Collections. His work is quiet, precise, and rooted in everyday utility.

"Every stitch is a decision. Every product, a story."

Khatoon Khadem – Conservator of Historical Artifacts (Dova)

Khatoon holds a degree in Conservation of Historical Artifacts, and her eye for detail and texture translates beautifully into her handmade bags under the brand Dova. Each bag — from ruffled pouches to structured totes — is sewn with care, using durable fabrics like Teragal and Tetrone. Her designs are soft, feminine, and timeless.

"Stitch by stitch, I preserve beauty — not just in history, but in daily use."
